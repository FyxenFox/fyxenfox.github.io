Georgy Zhukov

Leningrad, Moscow and Stalingrad. Three major defenses that ultimately defeated the Axis
powers. It takes a great man to be able to see such a devastation of your country to the
Axis powers and still fight them back all the way to Berlin. When I feel sad I think of
those trapped in Stalingrad, starving and facing certain defeat. It makes my worries seem trivial!
